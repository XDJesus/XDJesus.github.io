Q and A's On Adopting

  1. Are adoption fees expensive?
    2. While you do have to pay for the adoption of a pet and might cost you anywhere from 50$ to 200$ you are spending way less than if you purchased a dog from a breader for over 100$.
  2. Do you need to fill out an application to adopt?
    3. Yes you do. To find the perfect home for the pet and the perfect pet for you most adoption centers will need to know a few things about you.
  3. Can we meet the animals before adopting?
    4. It depends on the adoption agency you are working with. Some instances like Adoptapalooza is an event where you get to meet the animals right then and there. While other places have photos and have you fill out the application first.
  4. Are the animals vaccinated before we receive them?
    5. Many of the animals up for adoption are vaccinated which is why there is a fee to adopt them. When the animals are left at the shelters they get examined by vets to make sure everything is alright with them.
